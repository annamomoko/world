About Intersection
Intersection was an immersive light- and sound-based multimedia art installation where an underwater forest moved into a downtown alley.
The visitor was invited into a multisensory dream world where hidden messages from the sea were uncovered.
Intersection provided a place to pause, reflect, and feel, inviting the visitor on a journey of discovery between the artificial and natural, self and community, past and future.
